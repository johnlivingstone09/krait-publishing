Welcome To KRAIT Book Publishing Company

Krait Book Publishing is a dynamic and inclusive self publishing company dedicated to bringing stories, novels, and knowledge to life in every format. We offer a wide range of books including story books, history books, textbooks, coffee table books, recipe books, and regional language literature available in print publishing, audiobooks, e books, and digital book publishing.

Whether you're looking to read books, listen to audio books, or explore free ebooks, Krait ensures quality, creativity, and accessibility for readers of all ages. From traditional book publishing companies to modern digital publishing services, we make discovering books, publishing your book, and reading stories exciting and meaningful for everyone.
